Ether-1 Staking/Lending Help


Become an  ETHO  Lender

  1. 1. Login with your private key
  2. 2. Click “Add Contract”
  3. 3. Select desired fees and submit

Become an  ETHO  Borrower

  1. 1. Login with your private key
  2. 2. Select desired available contract
  3. 3. Confirm fee payment and submit to reserve contract
  4. 4. Setup your node at https://nodes.ether1.org
  5. 5. Use your staking dashboard to send node verification tx (click “Send TX” button) and enter verification address from node dashboard registration. Use resulting tx hash for verification

Ether-1 Staking/Lending Terms & Conditions


Terms & Conditions

  1. 1. Borrower and Lender agree they understand and agree to the terms & conditions below upon entering their private keys on this website.
  2. 2. After claiming an available staking contract, Borrower has 50,000 blocks to setup an active & paying Ether-1 node using Lender's smart contract stake. Failure to do so forfeits Borrower's safety deposit to the Lender.
  3. 3. When Borrower's Ether-1 node produces its first reward payment, contract status is changed from inactive to active. The contract status is changed back to inactive if the Borrower's node loses its paying status for a single payment period.
  4. 4. Borrower can cancel an active contract after 200,000 blocks with the safety deposit returned to the Borrower.
  5. 5. If Borrower cancels an active contract within the first 200,000 blocks, security deposit is forfeit to lender.
  6. 6. Lender can cancel an active contract only after 200,000 blocks, with the safety deposit returned to the Borrower.
  7.  - Lender has the option to stipulate additional Terms & Conditions within each staking contract. By claiming the staking contract, Borrower agrees to Lender's additional Term & Conditions.
  8.  - Lender's additional stipulated Terms & Conditions do not supersede Smart Contract rules (1) through (6) listed above.
